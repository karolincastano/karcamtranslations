Our agency

At KarCam Translations we work with passion so that our clients are satisfied and always use our services again. We are a team of translators passionate about our work. We always deliver quality, on time and trustworty translations. We have specialized native and bilingual translators. We use the most updated technology to translate according to the kind of assignments and thus we optimize our translation work.

History

We are an agency that started with a group of translation students in 2017. Thanks to sponsoring we managed to create a stable and recognized agency. We continue to grow at a steady pace, increasing our work-load, our equipment and our services.

We specialize in the following fields:

Translation

Scientific-technical and literary translations.

Revision and proofreading

Spelling, grammatical and style revision.

Audiovisual

Subtitling, dubbing and subtitles for people with hearing disabilities.

Localisation

Focused on video games and web pages localisation.
